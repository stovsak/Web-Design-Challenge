    • Latitude vs. Humidity

    Although there appears to be no direct relationship between the humidity and the latitude of a place, it is evident from the plot that most cities are situated in areas where the humidity is higher than 60%. Ancient civilizations emerged and developed mostly near the banks of rivers, lakes or seashores and the trend still remains. A strong possibility of why cities emerge close to water is the ease of obtain life sustaining capabilities water has for the people residing there. It is also observed that very few cities are spotted in areas with low humidity percentage.
